Orders

How do I place an order?
Simply browse our collection, select your favourite piece, add it to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Once your payment is confirmed, you'll receive an order confirmation via email.
Can I modify my order after placing it?
If your order hasn't been packed or dispatched yet, we'll do our best to accommodate your request. Please contact us as soon as possible after placing your order.
Can I cancel my order?
Orders can only be cancelled before they are dispatched. Once your order has been shipped, cancellations are no longer possible.
How will I know my order is confirmed?
As soon as your order is successfully placed, you'll receive a confirmation email with your order details.

Shipping

Do you deliver across India?
Yes! We deliver across most locations in India.
How long does delivery take?
Orders are usually processed within 1–3 business days. After dispatch, delivery generally takes 3–7 business days, depending on your location.

Please note that deliveries to remote locations may take slightly longer.
How can I track my order?
Once your order has been dispatched, you'll receive a tracking link via email or SMS so you can follow your package every step of the way.
What if my order is delayed?
While we work hard to deliver on time, occasional delays may occur due to weather conditions, courier issues, or unforeseen circumstances. If your order is significantly delayed, please contact our support team, and we'll be happy to assist you.

Returns & Exchanges

Do you accept returns?
No. Due to the nature of our products and hygiene considerations, all purchases made on CHAMKILI are final. We do not accept returns.
Do you offer exchanges?
No. We do not offer exchanges once an order has been delivered.
What if I receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect product?
We take great care in packing every order. However, if you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with your order number, clear photos, and an unboxing video. After verification, we'll work with you to provide an appropriate resolution.

Products

Is your jewellery anti-tarnish?

Is your jewellery suitable for sensitive skin?

Does the jewellery come in gift-ready packaging?
Yes! Every CHAMKILI order is thoughtfully packed, making it perfect for gifting a loved one or treating yourself.

Jewellery Care

How should I store my jewellery?
Store your jewellery in a dry place, preferably in the pouch or box provided, away from direct sunlight and moisture.
Can I wear my jewellery every day?
Many of our pieces are suitable for regular wear. To extend their lifespan, we recommend removing them before swimming, showering, exercising, or sleeping.
How do I clean my jewellery?
Use a soft, dry cloth to gently wipe your jewellery after each use. Avoid abrasive cleaners or harsh chemicals to help preserve its shine and finish.

Payments

Which payment methods do you accept?

Is Cash on Delivery (COD) available?
If Cash on Delivery (COD) is available, it will be shown as a payment option during checkout based on your delivery location.
Are my payments secure?
Absolutely. All payments are processed through secure and trusted payment gateways to ensure your personal and payment information remains protected.

Offers & Discounts

Can I use more than one discount code?
Only one promotional or discount code can be applied per order unless stated otherwise in the offer terms.
Why isn't my discount code working?
Discount codes may have expiry dates, minimum order value requirements, or product exclusions. Please review the offer terms and conditions before applying the code. If you're still experiencing issues, feel free to contact our customer support team for assistance.
